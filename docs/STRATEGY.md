# English Buddy — Strategy Document
## Sisense Builder Evangelist Assignment — April 2026

## The Narrative

"I'm a builder. I vibe-coded an AI-powered English tutor for kids using Claude. Now I need analytics before I go to market — I need to understand retention, engagement, and what content works. Sisense got me from zero to investor-ready analytics in one afternoon."

---

## Target Audience

**Primary:** Solo builders and indie developers who've shipped (or are about to ship) their first product and need analytics to:
- Understand if their product is working (retention, engagement)
- Impress investors with real KPIs
- Make data-driven decisions about what to improve
- A/B test different experiences

**Secondary:** EdTech builders specifically — anyone building AI-powered learning tools who needs to measure learning outcomes and engagement.

**Why this audience for Sisense:** These builders can code but don't want to spend a sprint building a custom analytics dashboard. They need something that plugs into their existing database and gives them answers fast.

---

## The Problem

You built the thing. Users are coming (or about to). But you're flying blind:
- Are kids coming back after day 1?
- Where in the experience do they drop off?
- Which content works best?
- What does your retention curve look like?
- Can you show an investor a cohort chart?

You need analytics NOW, not after a 2-week dashboard-building sprint.

---

## The Solution (Sisense)

Connect Sisense to your existing PostgreSQL. Get a retention dashboard, funnel visualization, and A/B test results in minutes — not days. Use Compose SDK to embed charts directly in your admin panel. Use the MCP Server to ask natural language questions about your data.
